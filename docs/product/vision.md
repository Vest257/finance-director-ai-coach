# Product Vision

FinanceOS will become a long-term simulation platform for aspiring and current finance leaders. Its first product is the Finance Director Scenario Coach, which will help learners practice judgment, communication, commercial thinking, governance, and leadership through realistic Finance Director and CFO scenarios.

## Long-Term Vision

The product should simulate the work of senior finance leadership across ambiguous business situations. Over time, learners should be able to:

- Work through realistic scenarios with evolving context.
- Analyze financial packs and business tradeoffs.
- Roleplay with stakeholders such as CEOs, founders, boards, auditors, lenders, and functional leaders.
- Receive feedback on both technical finance quality and leadership judgment.
- Track competency growth across repeated practice.
- Build confidence for real Finance Director and CFO responsibilities.

## Initial Quick Win

The first usable product should be the text-based Finance Director Scenario Coach. It should let a learner enter a scenario, respond to guided questions, skip to a model solution if needed, receive a debrief, see a qualitative competency scorecard, compare with a model Finance Director answer, and leave with a practical action plan.

This first version should prove the learning loop before investing in a frontend, database, external AI integration, or advanced simulation systems.

## Product Promise

The coach should help finance leaders practice decisions that are difficult to rehearse safely in real life:

- How to frame tradeoffs under uncertainty.
- How to challenge assumptions without damaging trust.
- How to balance cash, growth, governance, and stakeholder expectations.
- How to communicate financial insight clearly.
- How to develop from controller-level execution toward strategic finance leadership.

## Assumptions

- Learners benefit from repeated practice more than static finance content.
- The first version can deliver value without stored learner profiles.
- Text scenarios are enough to validate the core learning loop.
- Financial packs can begin as simple static inputs before becoming generated or interactive.
- AI roleplay is important later, but should not shape the first architecture before the scenario loop is understood.

## Success Direction

The product is successful if learners can point to better thinking patterns after using it, not merely completed lessons. Evidence of success should include improved answers, clearer financial communication, and stronger decision discipline across scenarios.
